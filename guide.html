Step 1

Convert the Zip file to a Folder



Step 2

Go to your browser and search the Extwnsions option

Step 3

After entering the manage extensions page Start the Developer Mode

After turning on the developer mode you will be able to see three options,Load Unpacked,Pack Extension and Update



Step 4

Click on load unpacked and then select the folder which you downloaded form our website
